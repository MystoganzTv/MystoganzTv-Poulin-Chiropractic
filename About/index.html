About Us

Background & Credentials

Dr. Poulin has dedicated his career to thoughtful, high-quality chiropractic care, with extensive training in Cox® Technic Flexion-Distraction and Decompression for patients seeking a more conservative path for complex spinal conditions.

The practice receives frequent referrals from neurosurgeons, pain management physicians, orthopedic surgeons, general practitioners, dentists, and physiatrists who value a careful, non-surgical approach.

  • ✓Part 3 Certified in Cox® Disc Decompression, a distinction held by a small percentage of chiropractors.
  • ✓Providing chiropractic care in Herndon since 1993, with a second location in Ashburn added in 2006.
  • ✓Graduate of Parker Chiropractic College, completing an accelerated doctoral program with a strong focus on clinical discipline.
  • ✓Former President of the Sports Council on Chiropractic at Parker.
  • ✓Equipped with advanced chiropractic technology, including Cox® treatment tables for decompression-focused care.
  • ✓Fluent in French and knowledgeable in Spanish, helping patients feel more comfortable and understood.
  • ✓Long-standing involvement in the Herndon and Ashburn community through local business and civic relationships.

A Practice Built on Trust

Poulin Chiropractic has grown through long-term patient relationships, specialist referrals, and a consistent reputation for conservative care that is calm, clear, and clinically grounded.

For many patients, the experience here is not only about relief, but about finally understanding what is happening in the spine and having a measured plan that feels responsible and realistic.

That combination of clinical experience, communication, and continuity is what continues to bring families back across Herndon, Ashburn, and the surrounding Northern Virginia communities.

In Memory of Dr. James Cox

Dr. James Cox was the founder of Cox® Technic and an important mentor to Dr. Poulin. His passing in March 2025 marked the loss of one of chiropractic’s most influential innovators, but his work continues through the patients and practitioners shaped by his method.

Dr. Poulin’s direct training relationship with Dr. Cox remains one of the defining strengths of this practice, reinforcing a standard of care rooted in technique, precision, and fidelity to the original clinical intent.
