Background & Credentials

Dr. Poulin has dedicated his career to providing the highest quality chiropractic care. His extensive training in Cox® Technic Flexion-Distraction and Decompression makes him uniquely qualified to treat complex spinal conditions without surgery. Our office receives frequent referrals from neurosurgeons, pain management doctors, orthopedic surgeons, general practitioners, dentists, and physiatrists.

  • ✓Part 3 Certified in Cox® Disc Decompression — less than 1% of chiropractors worldwide
  • ✓In practice in Herndon since 1993
  • ✓Opened Herndon practice in May 1997
  • ✓Opened second location in Ashburn in 2006
  • ✓Graduated Parker Chiropractic College (1989–1992) — finished 4-year doctorate in 3 years
  • ✓President of the Sports Council on Chiropractic at Parker
  • ✓State-of-the-art chiropractic equipment including Cox® 7th generation tables
  • ✓Fluent in French, knowledgeable in Spanish
  • ✓Member of the Herndon Chamber of Commerce and Herndon Arts Council

In Memory of Dr. James Cox

Dr. James Cox was the founder of Cox® Technic and a great friend and mentor to Dr. Poulin. He passed away in March 2025 at 84 years young. His legacy lives on through the countless patients who have benefited from his revolutionary treatment approach.
