What We Offer

Comprehensive services designed for calmer, more confident recovery.

Our Services

A more refined approach than a one-size-fits-all treatment plan.

Care here is built around the spine first, then supported by complementary therapies when they add value to healing, pain relief, tissue recovery, and long-term resilience. The result is a treatment plan that feels deliberate, conservative, and clinically grounded.

Care Philosophy

Treatment is chosen with purpose. The goal is not to overwhelm patients with modalities, but to use the right service at the right time in support of a clearer path forward.

Primary Service

Cox® Technic Flexion-Distraction remains the clinical foundation of the practice.

This is the core service for patients with disc problems, radiating pain, spinal stenosis, spondylolisthesis, chronic low back pain, neck pain, and cases where a non-surgical option is especially important.

Using a specialized Cox table, Dr. Poulin applies gentle, precise decompression intended to reduce intradiscal pressure, improve spinal motion, and calm irritation around affected nerves.

  • Research-documented approach for spine and disc-related complaints
  • Gentle decompression without forceful twisting or aggressive manipulation
  • Often considered by patients referred from surgeons and pain specialists
  • Used as the anchor for more personalized treatment plans
