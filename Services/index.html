The Treatment

What is Cox® Technic?

Named after its developer, James M. Cox DC DACBR, Cox® Technic Flexion Distraction and Decompression is a gentle, non-force chiropractic adjusting approach. It is a fusion of chiropractic and osteopathic principles, offering a non-surgical alternative as a first-line treatment.

The treatment is gentle, safe and directed — applied only to your tolerance. Your physician carefully watches your response every moment of application. It is guided by the "rule of 50%" ensuring as quick a release from care as possible.

Average Recovery Time

1,000 case studies found that low back pain patients found relief in an average of 12 visits over 29 days.
