Our Method

Cox® Technic Flexion-Distraction

The most researched chiropractic method for spine pain relief. Using a specialized table, Dr. Poulin applies gentle traction that decompresses spinal discs and nerves — no forceful cracking, no surgery.

  • Increases disc height and reduces intradiscal pressure
  • Decompresses spinal nerves to relieve radiating pain
  • Improves spinal mobility without aggressive manipulation
  • Backed by decades of published clinical research
  • Indicated for disc herniations, stenosis, spondylolisthesis & more
